REAL WORLD TOURISM

Yemen

Yemen is a land of ancient cities, unique architecture, and enduring traditions. From Shibam’s mud-brick towers to Sana’a’s historic alleys, it offers a glimpse into Arabia’s rich past. Despite challenges, Yemen’s people are resilient and welcoming, making it a truly memorable destination.

Libya

Libya offers dramatic deserts and rich history, from the Roman ruins of Leptis Magna to the golden Sahara dunes. Despite challenges, its people are proud and welcoming, making Libya a destination where history and hospitality meet.
